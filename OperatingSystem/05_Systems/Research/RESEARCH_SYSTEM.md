# Research System

**Purpose:** Make research repeatable and cumulative — area studies, feasibility, developer/project intel, market trends — so findings are filed, not lost.

**Owner:** [A08 Research & Feasibility Analyst](../../01_Agents/A08_Research_Feasibility_Analyst.md) · **Workflow:** [WF09](../../02_Workflows/WF09_Feasibility_Study.md)

## Method (local-first, sourced-always)
1. **Frame** the question and what decision it serves.
2. **Local first** — Property Master DB + `KnowledgeBase` area intelligence; normalize aliases, strip false areas (`PROPERTY_KNOWLEDGE.md`).
3. **External second** — only where local is thin. Every external figure carries **source + date**.
4. **Model / synthesize** with the right template ([T05 Feasibility](../../04_Templates/T05_Feasibility_Report.md), [T10 CMA](../../04_Templates/T10_Comparative_Market_Analysis.md)).
5. **Conclude** with one defensible call + the driving numbers + the key risk.
6. **File** the finding (below) and propose a durable knowledge entry to [A10](../../01_Agents/A10_Knowledge_Librarian.md).

## Filing
Save outputs as `Research_Findings/{{topic}}_{{YYYY-MM-DD}}.md`. Each finding records: question, sources (with dates), method, result, confidence, and the decision it informed. Durable area/developer rules graduate into the [Property/Operations playbooks](../../../KnowledgeBase/AIOS_Knowledge_Vault/category_playbooks/).

## Standing research targets (HSH-relevant)
- Area intelligence: Palm Jumeirah, Yas Island, Saadiyat, Al Reem, Al Raha, Al Reeman, JVC, Dubai growth corridors.

## Quality bar
- Comparables genuinely comparable. Every external number sourced + dated. One-line conclusion. Filed, not lost.
