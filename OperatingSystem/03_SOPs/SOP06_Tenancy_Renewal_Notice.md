# SOP06 — Tenancy Renewal & Notice

**Purpose:** Renew tenancies and serve rent-change / non-renewal / eviction notices within the legal windows.
**When:** A tenancy approaches expiry, or a rent change / non-renewal / eviction is intended.
**Owner:** [A06](../01_Agents/A06_Contracts_Renewals.md) · **Workflow:** [WF07](../02_Workflows/WF07_Contract_Renewal.md)

## Prerequisites / Documents
- Current tenancy contract + Ejari
- Landlord intent (renew as-is / change / non-renew / evict) + reason
- For rent increase: RERA rental index check
- For eviction: valid statutory ground + notary/registered-mail channel

## Steps
1. Confirm the expiry date and the applicable window.
2. **Rent change or non-renewal → serve 90 days' written notice** before expiry (`LEGAL_KNOWLEDGE.md` #5).
3. **Eviction (personal use / renovation) → serve 12 months' notice via notary or registered mail** (`LEGAL_KNOWLEDGE.md` #6).
4. Validate any increase against the RERA rental increase calculator.
5. Draft the notice/renewal ([T07](../04_Templates/T07_Renewal_Notice.md)); state window + service method.
6. 🔒 Omar serves the notice / signs the renewal.
7. New tenancy → re-Ejari within 30 days ([SOP03](SOP03_Ejari_Registration.md)); set the next renewal radar.

## Fees & Deadlines
- **90 days** notice for rent change / non-renewal
- **12 months** notice for personal-use/renovation eviction (notary/registered mail)
- Re-Ejari within **30 days** of the new contract

## Outputs
- Compliant notice/renewal served + recorded; re-Ejari triggered; next radar set.

## Risk-Hold points
- 🔒 Serving any notice. 🔒 Any eviction or dispute matter → legal review.
