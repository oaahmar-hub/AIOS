# SOP02 — DLD Transfer & Registration

**Purpose:** Execute and register a property ownership transfer at the DLD/registration trustee.
**When:** A sale is agreed and ready to transfer.
**Owner:** [A04](../01_Agents/A04_Compliance_Officer.md) · **Workflow:** [WF05](../02_Workflows/WF05_DLD_Transfer.md)

## Prerequisites / Documents
- Signed MOU (Form F) + broker forms (A seller / B buyer / I agency) as applicable
- Original title deed
- Buyer + seller passports / Emirates IDs (POA if represented)
- Developer NOC (for resales — see [SOP01](SOP01_Nakheel_NOC_Modification.md))
- Mortgage discharge / liability letter (if seller has a mortgage)
- Buyer mortgage pre-approval + bank valuation (if buyer financed)
- Manager's cheques (transfer amount, DLD fees, agency commission)

## Steps
1. Confirm MOU + forms signed and the deposit cheque held.
2. Trigger developer NOC if required; confirm service charge clearance.
3. Build the cost sheet (see Fees) and the document checklist.
4. Sequence mortgage settlement/discharge (seller) or bank handling (buyer) if financed.
5. Book the registration trustee office appointment; assemble all cheques + originals.
6. 🔒 Transfer day: signing, cheque handover, money movement (Omar executes/attends).
7. New title deed issued → file to Drive, update CRM/project register, trigger [WF08 Handover](../02_Workflows/WF08_Unit_Handover.md).

## Fees & Deadlines (verify live before cheques)
- **DLD transfer fee: 4% of purchase price** + trustee/admin/title-deed charges (`LEGAL_KNOWLEDGE.md` #1)
- **Mortgage registration: 0.25% of loan amount** + admin (if financed) (`LEGAL_KNOWLEDGE.md` #2)
- Agency commission per agreement
- Appointment-driven; no fixed statutory deadline once agreed, but cheques have validity windows

## Outputs
- Registered transfer + new title deed, settled fees, updated records, handover triggered.

## Risk-Hold points
- 🔒 All money movement, cheque handover, and signing.
