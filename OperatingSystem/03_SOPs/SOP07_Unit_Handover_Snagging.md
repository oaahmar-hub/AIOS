# SOP07 — Unit Handover & Snagging

**Purpose:** Hand over a unit cleanly and documented, with a thorough snag list and full transfer of utilities/access.
**When:** Developer→buyer, owner→tenant handover, or tenant move-out.
**Owner:** [A07](../01_Agents/A07_Handover_Coordinator.md) · **Workflow:** [WF08](../02_Workflows/WF08_Unit_Handover.md)

## Prerequisites / Documents
- Payment/clearance confirmation (before keys)
- Title deed / Oqood (sale) or signed tenancy + Ejari (rental)
- Handover checklist ([T06](../04_Templates/T06_Handover_Checklist.md))
- Camera for snag photos; meter-reading capture

## Steps
1. Confirm payment/clearance status before scheduling key release.
2. **Snag room-by-room**: doors, windows, walls, floors, ceilings, paint, AC, plumbing, electrical, fixtures, appliances, balcony. Photograph each defect. Categorize cosmetic / functional / safety; assign owner + deadline.
3. **Utilities**: activate/transfer DEWA (Dubai) / ADDC (Abu Dhabi) / chiller (Empower); record meter readings with date + photo.
4. **Access**: keys, fobs, parking, access cards, gate/community registration.
5. **Documents**: title deed/Oqood, Ejari, warranties, manuals handed over.
6. 🔒 Release keys only after clearance confirmed by Omar.
7. **Sign-off**: both parties acknowledge condition + snag list + readings; capture signatures.
8. Log to project register; track open snags to closure.

## Fees & Deadlines
- Utility deposits/activation fees (DEWA/ADDC/Empower)
- Snag resolution deadlines per category (safety = immediate)

## Outputs
- Handover pack, categorized photo-backed snag list, utility/access/Ejari status, signed condition record + meter readings.

## Risk-Hold points
- 🔒 Keys before clearance. 🔒 Sign-off with unresolved safety/functional defects. 🔒 Disputed condition.
