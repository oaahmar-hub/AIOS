# SOP09 — Client Onboarding

**Purpose:** Convert a new contact into a properly recorded, qualified, compliant client relationship.
**When:** A new buyer/seller/landlord/tenant enters the pipeline.
**Owner:** [A02](../01_Agents/A02_Deal_Closer.md) · **Supports:** A04

## Prerequisites / Documents
- Contact details + source
- ID for KYC (when transacting)
- Qualification info ([T02](../04_Templates/T02_Client_Qualification.md))

## Steps
1. **Create the CRM record** (contact + lead) with source attribution.
2. **Qualify** the 6 fields: area, budget, beds/type, timeline, purpose, cash/mortgage.
3. **Classify** the client type and DNA (buyer/seller/landlord/tenant/investor) for tailored handling.
4. **KYC trigger** — when the relationship moves toward a transaction, start [SOP08](SOP08_AML_KYC_Compliance.md).
5. **Set the relationship cadence** — assign a follow-up rhythm; every client has a next touch.
6. **Welcome touch** — premium, short intro in Omar's voice; set expectations.
7. **Log** to CRM + pipeline tracker; set the first follow-up task.

## Fees & Deadlines
- None at onboarding; KYC retention applies once transacting.

## Outputs
- CRM contact + lead, qualification, client classification, first follow-up task.

## Risk-Hold points
- 🔒 Storing/sharing client PII outside approved systems.
