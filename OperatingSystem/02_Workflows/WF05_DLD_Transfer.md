# WF05 — DLD Transfer

**Flow:** Sale agreed → NOC → fees & docs → trustee appointment → transfer → register
**Trigger:** A sale is agreed and the transfer must be executed.
**Lead agent:** [A04 Compliance Officer](../01_Agents/A04_Compliance_Officer.md) · **Supports:** A05, A06, A07

## Steps
1. **Form & MOU** — confirm signed MOU (Form F) and broker forms (A/B/I) are in place → [A06](../01_Agents/A06_Contracts_Renewals.md).
2. **Developer NOC** — trigger [WF04](WF04_Nakheel_NOC_Submission.md) if a NOC is required (most resales). Needs service charge clearance.
3. **Fees** — compute: DLD transfer **4%** + trustee/admin/title-deed; mortgage registration **0.25%** of loan if financed; agency commission. Produce the cost sheet.
4. **Document checklist** ([SOP02](../03_SOPs/SOP02_DLD_Transfer_Registration.md)): title deed, IDs/passports, NOC, MOU, mortgage discharge (if any), manager's cheques.
5. **Mortgage** — if seller financed, sequence the bank settlement/discharge; if buyer financed, the bank's pre-approval + valuation.
6. **Trustee appointment** — book the registration trustee office slot; assemble cheques.
7. 🔒 **Transfer day** — money movement, cheque handover, signing → Omar executes/attends.
8. **Register & close** — new title deed issued; update CRM/project register; trigger [WF08 Handover](WF08_Unit_Handover.md).

## Outputs
- Cost sheet, document checklist (✓/gaps), NOC status, trustee booking, post-transfer title deed logged.

## Done when
- Title transferred and registered, fees settled, CRM/register updated, handover triggered.

## Note
- Every fee figure must be re-verified against the live DLD schedule before the cheques are cut (A04 verify-before-contract flag).
