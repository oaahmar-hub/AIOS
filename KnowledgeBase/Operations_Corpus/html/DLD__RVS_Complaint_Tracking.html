RVS Complaint Tracking

Description

This service enables customers to inquire about the status of a Real Estate Violation System complaint number

Access this Service
  • Service Procedures
  • Service Time
  • Service Channels
  • Issued Documents
Service Procedures

Via the website or App
Step I: Log in to the website or the App and select the service.
Step II: Enter the required inquiry and click submit.
Step III: Receive a response on the status of the case displayed on the screen

Service Time

Immediate

Service Channels

1)Land Department website
2)Dubai REST App

Issued Documents

A downloadable PDF document or report on the closure status of the Complaint
