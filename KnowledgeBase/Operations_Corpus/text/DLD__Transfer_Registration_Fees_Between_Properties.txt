Read More Access this Service Expand All Service Procedures Required Documents Service Fees Service Time Service Terms Service Channels Issued Documents Payment Method Residency Status Service Procedures Step I: Login to Oqood portal and select registration from one property to another service Step II: Selecting a property, filling in the details, attaching the documents, and selecting the payment method Step III: Sending the application on-line Step IV: Receipt of the output via an e-mail message sent to the purchaser Required Documents 1- A letter or a copy of the e-mail sent by the Legal Affairs Department indicating approval 2- The details table, which contains the details of the deregistered property and the property to be registered 3- The signed contract after printing on the system, to be attached before sending (a copy of the settlement agreement signed by the two parties) 4- The new sale and purchase agreement 5- UAE ID 6- A valid passport for non-residents 7- Old title deeds to be transferred Service Fees The seller: 2% of the sale value The purchaser: 2% of the sale value AED 10 Knowledge fees AED 10 Innovation fees Self-registration fees for developers: - (Oqood Portal - Real Estate Developers Portal) for provisional sale AED 1000 fee Fees shall be charged in case of an increase in the price of the property transferred to, as the fees shall be charged for the price increase only Service Time 6 business days Service Terms 1- Fees shall be charged in case of an increase of the price of the property transferred to, as the fees shall be charged for the price increase only Service Channels The Land Department's website (Real Estate Developers Portal -
