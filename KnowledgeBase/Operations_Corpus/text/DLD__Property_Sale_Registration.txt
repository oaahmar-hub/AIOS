Any deficiencies are identified and addressed. All required documents are uploaded via the digital vault. Step 2: The employee enters the transaction data into the system and performs an audit. Step 3: Pay the fees and receive a receipt for payment. The output is sent via email. Step 4: Enter the buyer’s information either by Emirates ID or passport, then click Submit. Step 5: Your request has now been successfully created. Please note the reference number for booking, as it will be needed for follow-up. You can check the status of your request under “My Requests”. Required Documents Individuals: 1- Emirates ID of both the seller and buyer (for identity verification only; no copies are taken) Or a valid passport for non-resident foreignersز 2- A no-objection e-certificate (e-NOC) from the developer in the freehold areas (via Dubai REST App) Service Fees Seller: 2% of the sale value Buyer: 2% of the sale value Additional Fees: AED 250 Title Deed Certificate Issuance Fee AED 225 Unified Map under Dubai Municipality AED 100 Map for lands not under Dubai Municipality AED 250 Villas and Apartments AED 10 knowledge fee AED 10 innovation fee Service Partner Fees: AED 4,000 + VAT If the sale value is AED 500,000 or more AED 2,000 + VAT If the sale value is less than AED 500,000 Service Time 25 minutes Service Terms The company must be registered by submitting a company registration request Service Form E-form Service Channels Real Estate Registration Trustees Center Issued Documents Electronic Title Deed Electronic Map Fee Balances Payment Method ePay Dubai Pay Noqodi Wallet Manager Cheque Third Parties / Partners Real Estate Trustee Centers Residency Status All Facebook Linkedin Instagram Twitter Email About Us Support Complaint Direction Frequently Asked Questions Customer Happiness Charter Terms and Conditions Privacy Policy Accessibility Sitemap Service Name Updates This site is best viewed on latest versions of Chrome / Firefox / Edge -
