5. **Knowledge corpus build** — A local corpus was created from official sources across DLD, RERA, Tarakheesi, ICP, GDRFA, mortgages, and NOC pages. (Source: Operations corpus build)
6. **Property master database** — A normalized property database was built for search and ranking of inventory rows. (Source: Database build)
