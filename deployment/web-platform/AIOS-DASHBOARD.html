Week 1 — Foundation
Start here: Activate 3 core agents immediately
Open agents/01-NOC-APPROVALS-AGENT.md, 02-DLD-EJARI-AGENT.md, and 04-CLIENT-MANAGER-AGENT.md. Apply them to your first 3 real cases this week. Use this dashboard as your daily reference.
⏱ 1 week to operational
2
Week 2 — Pipeline & Listings
Set up Pipeline + Property Finder systems
Create your first pipeline entries in PROJECTS.md. Apply the Property Finder agent to your current active listings — audit and optimize each one. Run the weekly review for the first time.
⏱ End of week 2
3
Week 3 — Finance & Marketing
Activate Finance + Marketing agents
Issue your first AIOS-generated invoice. Create your first marketing broadcast using the Marketing agent. Set your monthly revenue target in Finance agent.
⏱ End of week 3
4
Month 2 — Full Activation
All 10 agents in regular use
Every business process routes through the system. Daily planner in use every morning. Weekly review every Sunday. Decision log updated weekly. All templates in active use.
⏱ End of month 2
5
Month 3 — Knowledge Base
Build your personal knowledge layer
Add your actual community data, service charges, developer contacts, client intelligence records, and brand-specific templates to the operating system.

1. Open the AIOS folder — 22 files are ready in your outputs directory.
