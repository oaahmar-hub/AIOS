# AIOS Privacy Policy Draft

AIOS is a private business operating system for HSH Global Dubai.

AIOS may process business messages, property requirements, client communications, documents, tasks, calendar information, and operational records to provide command center, search, workflow, and communication features.

AIOS does not sell personal data.

AIOS may use trusted service providers for hosting, automation, AI processing, storage, communications, and analytics where required to operate the service.

Restricted data such as owner contact details, private documents, internal CRM notes, internal commissions, and confidential deal information is protected by AIOS permission rules and is not exposed to unauthorized users.

Users may request deletion or correction of data by contacting HSH Global Dubai.

This draft must be reviewed before App Store or Google Play submission.
