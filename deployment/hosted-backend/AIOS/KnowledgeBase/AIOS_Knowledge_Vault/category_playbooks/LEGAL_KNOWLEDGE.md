# Legal Knowledge Playbook

Core Dubai real estate regulatory memory for transfers, Ejari, RERA, and AML.

## Extracted Knowledge
1. **DLD transfer fee** — Standard transfer fee is 4% of purchase price plus trustee/admin/title deed charges. (Source: DLD fee schedule)
2. **Mortgage registration** — Mortgage registration is 0.25% of the loan amount plus admin fee. (Source: DLD fee schedule)
3. **RERA advertising permit** — A RERA permit is required before publishing paid listings. (Source: RERA quick reference)
4. **Ejari deadline** — Tenancy contracts must be registered in Ejari within 30 days of signing. (Source: RERA quick reference)
5. **Notice period** — Rent changes or non-renewal require 90 days written notice. (Source: RERA quick reference)
6. **Eviction notice** — Personal-use or renovation eviction requires 12 months notice served through a notary or registered mail. (Source: RERA quick reference)
7. **AML retention** — KYC and transaction records must be retained for 5 years. (Source: RERA quick reference)
8. **goAML reporting** — Suspicious transactions must be escalated and filed within the required AML window. (Source: RERA quick reference)
9. **Golden visa property threshold** — Golden residency path in the corpus uses a property ownership threshold around AED 2 million without loans. (Source: ICP and DLD corpus)
10. **NOC prerequisites** — Typical developer NOC requests need title deed, passport/EID, service charge clearance, and scope details. (Source: NOC and transfer corpus)
