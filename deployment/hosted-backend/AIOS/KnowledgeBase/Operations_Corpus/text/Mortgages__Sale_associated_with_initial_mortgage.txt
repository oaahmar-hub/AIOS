Read More Access this Service Expand All Service Procedures Required Documents Service Fees Service Time Service Terms Service Channels Issued Documents Payment Method Third Parties / Partners Residency Status Service Procedures Step I: Login to Oqood portal and select provisional sale accompanied with mortgage registration service Step II: Selecting a property, filling in the details, attaching the documents, and selecting the payment method Step III: Sending the application on-line Step IV: Receipt of the output via an e-mail message sent to the purchaser Required Documents Individuals 1- A copy of the sale and purchase contract 2- A copy of a valid UAE ID 3- A copy of a valid passport for non-residents 4- A letter from the bank indicating the value and date of the mortgage and the three mortgage contracts signed by both parties Companies ( Individual (One person) establishment) 1- A copy of a valid trade license 2- A copy of the UAE ID or a valid passport of the license holder 3- Power of attorney, if any ( Limited Liability Companies ) 1- A copy of a valid trade license 2- A copy of the UAE ID or a valid passport of the license holder 3- Power of attorney, if any 4- A copy of the company’s Memorandum of Association and the annexes thereof (legal translation into Arabic language) 5- A copy of the shareholder certificate. ( Foreign company) 1- A copy of a valid trade license 2- A copy of the UAE ID or a valid passport of the license holder 3- Power of attorney, if any 4- A copy of the translated company’s Memorandum of Association and the annexes thereof, (legal translation into Arabic language) attested by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs 5- A no objection letter (NOC) from the free zones, in case of purchase, valid for one year 6- A copy of the shareholder certificate. ( GCC company ) 1- A copy of a valid trade license 2- A copy of the national ID or passport of the license holder 3- Power of attorney, if any 4- A copy of the translated company’s Memorandum of Association and the annexes thereof, (legal translation into Arabic language) attested by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. 5- A copy of the shareholder certificate. Service Fees The seller: 2% of the sale value The purchaser: 2% of the sale value AED 10 Knowledge fees AED 10 Innovation fees title deed fee: AED 250 fee for an apartment or villas and land Map fee: AED 250 fee for a real estate unit or villa AED 100 for a land map for lands outside of the jurisdiction of Dubai Municipality AED 225 fees for the land plot map unified with Dubai Municipality. AED 10 Knowledge fees AED 10 Innovation fees Self-registration fees for developers: - (Oqood Portal - Real Estate Developers Portal) for provisional sale AED 1000 fee Mortgage fee: 0.25% of the mortgage value Service Time 6 business days Service Terms 1- The sale and purchase contract shall be signed by the developer and the purchase 2- In case that the purchase is a minor, the sale and purchase contract shall be signed by the guardian and a copy of the guardian’s passport or ID shall be attached. 3- The sale and purchase contract shall be registered in the provisional register within 90 days from the date of signing the contract 4- In case that the purchase is a decree holder person, a valid letter to To Whom It May Concern issued by the Federal Authority for Identity and UAE citizenship shall be attached (it required to attach an ID of another nationality) In the case of an Foreign company, it shall be registered in the free zones (Dubai Multi Commodities Center Authority (DMCC) or Jebel Ali Free Zone Authority (JAFZA)) Service Channels The Land Department's website (Real Estate Developers Portal -
