Read More Access this Service Expand All Service Procedures Required Documents Service Fees Service Time Service Terms Service Channels Issued Documents Payment Method Residency Status Service Procedures Step I: Login to Oqood portal and select provisional sale registration service Step II: Selecting a property, filling in the details, attaching the documents, and selecting the payment method Step III: Sending the application on-line Step IV: Receipt of the output via an e-mail message sent to the purchaser Required Documents Individuals 1- A copy of the sale and purchase contract 2- A copy of a valid UAE ID 3- A copy of a valid passport for non-residents Companies ( Individual (One person) establishment ) 1- A copy of a valid trade license 2- A copy of the UAE ID or passport of the license holder 3- Power of attorney, if any ( Limited Liability Companies ) 1- A copy of a valid trade license 2- A copy of the UAE ID or passport of the license holder 3- Power of attorney, if any 4- A copy of the translated company’s Memorandum of Association and the annexes thereof (legal translation into Arabic language) 5- A copy of the shareholder certificate ( An Foreign company ) ( provided that the names of the shareholders are prescribed ) 1- A copy of a valid trade license 2- A copy of the national ID or passport of the license holder 3- Power of attorney, if any 4- A copy of the translated company’s Memorandum of Association and the annexes thereof, (legal translation into Arabic language) ratified by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs 5- A no objection letter (NOC) from the free zones, in case of purchase contract, valid for one year. 6- A copy of the shareholder certificate. ( GCC company ) 1- A copy of a valid trade license 2- A copy of the national ID or passport of the license holder 3- Power of attorney, if any 4- A copy of the translated company’s Memorandum of Association and the annexes thereof, (legal translation into Arabic language) ratified by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. 5- A copy of the shareholder certificate. Service Fees The seller: 2% of the sale value The purchaser: 2% of the sale value AED 10 Knowledge fees AED 10 Innovation fees Self-registration fees for developers: - (Oqood Portal - Real Estate Developers Portal) for Provisional sale AED 1000 fee Service Time Business day Service Terms 1- The sale and purchase contract shall be signed by the developer and the purchaser 2- In case that the purchaser is a minor, the sale and purchase contract shall be signed by the guardian and a copy of the guardian’s passport or ID shall be attached. 3- The sale and purchase contract shall be registered in the provisional register within 90 days from the date of signing the contract 4- In case that the purchaser is a decree holder person, a valid letter to To Whom It May Concern issued by the Federal Authority for Identity and UAE citizenship shall be attached.
