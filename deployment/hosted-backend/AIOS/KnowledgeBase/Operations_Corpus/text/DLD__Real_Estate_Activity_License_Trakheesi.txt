Annual Fee AED 20 Knowledge and Innovation Fee Service Time 1 Working Day Service Terms 1- Submit the license application through the Dubai Department of Economy and Tourism. 2- Register in the Trakheesi system via the Dubai Land Department website: www.dubailand.gov.ae. 3- Apply for a Real Estate Activity Practice Card immediately after submitting and receiving approval for the real estate license. Free Zone Licenses: - Must obtain a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the licensing authority. Real Estate Development Licenses: - Must provide ownership of the land to be developed. - Ownership must be under the license holder or one of the partners. - Ownership data is verified electronically via the system. - To market the project, an official permit must be obtained from the Trakheesi system. Real Estate Registration Trustee Real Estate Services Trustee Real Estate Promotion Trustee Real Estate Service Center → Require signing an agreement between the Dubai Land Department and the license holder. Licenses for Administrative Supervision Services for Jointly Owned Property Management Companies: - Must register the license and relevant staff with the Real Estate Regulatory Agency (RERA). - Staff must obtain registration cards before practicing the activity. Licenses for Private Property Leasing and Management Services: - Must provide a copy of the property ownership deed. - Ownership must be under the license holder or one of the partners. - Allowed to manage properties of the license holder and second-degree relatives only, as per Local Order No. 2/2003. Licenses for Leasing and Managing Third-Party Properties: - Must provide a bank guarantee of AED 5 million. - If opening a branch, the trade name must match the parent license. - The branch must have at least 20 administrative employees.
